How We Began

It All Started With One Little Kitten…

It was May 2013. Friend Shelter volunteer got a call from a woman who caught a kitten that was in really bad shape. In an hour, the kitten made it to the vet and from that day forward, Alesya nurtured him back to health, and started the Cat Center Friend.

She named the kitten Rastishka. He was the Cat Center’s first foster disabled kitten. Currently as of 2020, the Dnipro Cat Center Friend has rescued, fostered and rehomed over 1000 cats. The shelter is also the permanent home of around 30 special needs cats with extreme disabilities.

Stray Cat Rehabilitation Centre

In 2017 volonteers started to build a Cat House. It is the country's only cat rehabilitation centre of such a high level. This house is specially equipped for cats with viral infections who are not accepted by veterinary hospitals or taken home by volunteers because of a high chance to infect other animals.

It gives a chance of survival to hundreds of hit, wounded, injured, disabled, old and ill cats from our country's streets… the cats who got into trouble and have no one to help them except for people with big hearts. More than a thousand cats and kittens have been taken from the street, cured and given to families for years of existence of our rehabilitation centre.

The Cat Center same as our Dog Shelter don't have government support. The shelter was created and operates only thanks to the voluntary work and enthusiasm of a few people who love animals.
